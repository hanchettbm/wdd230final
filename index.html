Our Purpose:

The Aurora Chamber of Commerce is an action-oriented membership organization that serves as the leading business voice in the Eastern Metro area. For our members, we seek to provide access, advocacy, and influence. We offer a variety of membership benefits and serve as a resource for connections with City, County, State, and Federal government contacts. Becoming active in the Aurora Chamber of Commerce is an excellent way to meet other business owners and business professionals.

Our goals are to help to further the interests of small businesses in a local area. We do this through activities that can range from hosting events, to lobbying local representatives to charitable works for the betterment of the community.
