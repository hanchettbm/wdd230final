Fun Facts For Small Towns

by Baden Hanchett

Small town living isn't just about the weather, it's also about the activities and expirences you can have as you visit these beautiful areas. For example Soda Springs has lots of fun sights. The Soda Springs Gyser is a great stop. If the weather is right, it feels like a hometown version of Old Faithful, but without the crowd of tourists. You can also ski in Kelly Park once you check for fresh powder! Preston Idaho is full of pioneer history and cultre. It's also the home of the great Napolean Dynamite. Fish Haven is right on the edge of bear lake. Take a swim! There are also many hinking trails like the Crimsion Trail and even the Minnetonka Caves to visit. Come and enjoy small town Idaho, whatever the weather may be.
